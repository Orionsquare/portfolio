About me:

I started my journey learning computer programming when I was 18. The first course I undertook was BASIC programming. This involved learning DOS commands. As computers were not at all popular back in those days (in the late 1980s), I did not progress my course further. Life caught up with me and I eventually went on to study law.

Things changed for me in 2020 (and I guess it must have changed for many as well!), as the community proceeded through mandatory lockdowns and and thoe who could work from home did. Consequently, I spent a load of time working on my computer. My interest in computer programming was re-ignited.

I aspire to be an Engineer and I am undertaking Full Stack Engineer Course to achieve my goal.
